What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by the websites you visit. They help a site work properly, stay secure, remember preferences and measure usage.

How we use cookies

  • Essential (required) — authentication, session management, CSRF protection.
  • Preferences — UI settings such as theme or language.
  • Analytics (optional) — aggregated metrics to improve performance and content.

We do not use cookies to sell personal information. Analytics, if enabled, are aggregated and anonymized where possible.

Retention

Session cookies expire when you close the browser; persistent cookies last from 1 day to 12 months unless deleted earlier.

Your choices

  • Use your browser controls to block or delete cookies (see Help for Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari).
  • If you block essential cookies, some features (login, posting) may not work.
  • You can opt out of analytics cookies in your browser or by using built-in tracking protection.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date reflects the latest version.
