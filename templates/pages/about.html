About us

Aurora is a tiny publishing platform for short, readable articles about software, web development and product craft. We focus on clarity, speed and accessibility.

What we publish

  • Backend: Python & Django patterns, APIs, testing, deploy.
  • Frontend: semantic HTML, accessible components, performance.
  • Product & UX: research shortcuts, onboarding, release notes.

How we write

  • Concise, practical, step-by-step guides.
  • Real code snippets and checklists you can apply fast.
  • Plain language, small paragraphs, scannable structure.

Get in touch

Want to propose a topic or contribute a draft? Contact us — we welcome concise, useful pieces that help readers ship faster.

Our principles

We optimize for reader time. Each article aims to answer a focused question, provide a minimal working example, and link to deeper resources. If a tutorial exceeds 7–10 minutes, we split it into parts.

Why readers trust Aurora

  • Editorial review for accuracy and clarity.
  • Consistent structure, clear headings and internal linking.
  • Fast pages: lightweight CSS, compressed media, accessible markup.
